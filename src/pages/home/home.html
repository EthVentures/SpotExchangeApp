Spot Exchange

The Challenge

Master Blockchain, Machine Learning, and Industry APIs and Open Source Technology while competing in a four-month virtual hackathon.

Our Pitch

We are building The Spot Exchange: a smart, decentralized, blockchain-based marketplace powered by Hyperledger Fabric and the IBM Z platform. The Spot Exchange enables people to rent physical locations designated for parking traditional and autonomous vehicles. Parking spaces include garages, lots, alleyways, and driveways, which may be located on private residential and commercial property.

Our Submission

  • The Distributed Marketplace - Hyperledger Fabric (github)
  • Our Mobile Front End - Ionic, Open source mobile SDK built on Angular (github)
  • Business Intelligence - Machine Learning Powered by IBM Watson (Price Recommendations) - IBM Watson (github)

Security and Privacy

  • Identity management/registration and SSO implemented through Auth0 (TBC - Phase III) (link)

Integrations
